About the game

Welcome to Click O'clock – the ultimate test of your speed and precision! Your mission is simple: click on the objects that appear before they vanish. Every successful click scores you points, and the faster you are, the higher your score!

No complicated rules, no stress—just a fun little challenge to see how fast you really are. Play for a high score, beat your own record, or just pass the time. Ready to give it a go? Click away and see how far you can get!
