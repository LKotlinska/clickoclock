About the game

Welcome to Click O'clock – a browser game and the ultimate test of your speed and precision! Your mission is simple: click on the objects before they disappear. Every successful click scores you points, but make sure you're accurate!

No complicated rules, just a fun little challenge to see how fast you really are. Play for a high score, beat your own record, or just pass the time. Ready to give it a go? Click away and see how far you can get!
